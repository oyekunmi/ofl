OUR GOAL

Informed Decisions

You have relevant and significant information, in a transparent manner, at your disposal to guide your decision and in the process you are protected from making impromptu buy decision which you could have made due to the absence of quality information.

Statement of guiding principles

our principles

We highlight the following principles guiding the operations of our comparison website:

Data source

we collect data directly from the product providers who are not, in any way, affiliated to us. Our commitment is to ensure product features are updated immediately we are notified of or notice a change and that information we publish is correct, complete and up-to-date, and from reliable data sources.

Free access

we are a free to use data-driven comparison site. Users are not charged for using our services neither are we remunerated nor funded by any of the providers.

Independent

we are independent of product providers; we do not directly sell products ourselves neither do we endorse nor encourage the use of any specific product provider.

Impartial

we are unbiased in our services. We strive to ensure our data yields robust results and product features are listed consistently and comprehensively across the various providers.
